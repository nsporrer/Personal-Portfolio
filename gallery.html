NEW ZEALAND INFO BOOK

ISU art 326—fall 2023
adobe indesign / adobe illustrator
7.5"x7.5"

This project focused on creating an information booklet for a country of my choice. The focus was on creating consistent and nice looking icons and graphs to illustrate a lot of information about the country. I chose to base my design on the Māori flag, which is the flag that represents the indigenous people of the region.

LEGO PRODUCTION POSTER

ISU art 326—fall 2023
adobe illustrator
18"x24"

This project emphasized the idea of providing information without using any words. I selected LEGO as my topic because it has an interesting creation process, and it allowed practice for designing within a brand's pre-determined style.

PORTFOLIO SHOW INVITATION

ICC grdsn 240—spring 2023
adobe illustrator / adobe indesign
5"x7"

This project involved the creation of an invitation to our school's portfolio show. The design was based on the word “Inspiration”. The cover is a photo with sketches that inspire me behind a computer that is in the process of designing. The back of the card contains the where, when, and who of the event with elements from the cover.

URBAN O2 SEED PACKAGING

ICC grdsn 150—fall 2022
adobe illustrator

This project included packaging design based around the company Burpee and their need for a new seed product focused on sustainability. I landed on the idea of high oxygen producing plants that are easy to grow for CO2 conscious people living in cities.

ICC DISC GOLF BRANDING

ICC grdsn 240—spring 2023
adobe illustrator

This project focused on the rebranding of the Illinois Central College disc golf course, and all of its elements. Including the logo, the baskets, parking signs, the hole maps, and the overall map of the course.

FUTURA FONT BROCHURE

ICC grdsn 150—fall 2022
adobe illustrator
5.33x8” tri-fold

This project consisted of research on a font, its designer, and the era's style in which it was created. The brochure created is focused on the Futura typeface, and incorporated design inspiration from the Bauhaus movement and the International Typo- graphic Style, which were both prominent movements during the creation of Futura.
